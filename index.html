Welcome To The College of New Jersey's...

College Union Board




The College Union Board...

... also known as CUB, organizes and sponsors community events here at the College of New Jersey. Including everything from concerts to comedy shows, fundraisers to free events, as well as late night activities and "nooner"s around campus where students can come participate in free activities in between classes. Our goal is provide a united and extraordinary campus experience for students and staff alike.
